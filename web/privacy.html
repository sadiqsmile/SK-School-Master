Privacy Policy

SK School Master • Last updated: 2026-03-08

This Privacy Policy explains how SK School Master ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the SK School Master application (the "App").

Note: This policy should be reviewed and updated to match your organization details (company name, address, contact email) and your actual data flows.

1) What information we collect

Account and school information

  • User account details (such as email address or phone number) used for authentication.
  • Role and school membership (e.g., super admin, school admin, teacher, parent).

School operational data

  • Student and staff information entered by authorized school admins (e.g., student name, admission number, class/section).
  • Attendance, fees, homework, announcements, and exam records created by authorized users.

Device and diagnostic data

  • Basic device information and app diagnostics (for security, reliability, and debugging).

2) How we use information

  • To provide the App features (school administration and reporting).
  • To authenticate users and enforce role-based access controls.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.

3) How we store and protect information

We use Firebase (Google) services such as Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Cloud Functions. We apply role-based security rules to limit access to school data.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We continually improve safeguards, but we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4) Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only:

  • With service providers necessary to operate the App (e.g., Firebase).
  • When required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

5) Children’s privacy

The App is designed for schools and is intended to be used by school staff and parents/guardians. Schools are responsible for ensuring they have the appropriate rights and consents to manage student data.

6) Your choices

  • You can request corrections to inaccurate school records through your school administrator.
  • Account access is controlled by school administration and role assignments.
